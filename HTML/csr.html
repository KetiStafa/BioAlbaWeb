Empowering Communities and Nurturing Sustainability

At BioAlba, we are more than just an organic medicinal plants producer; we are committed to making a positive impact on communities and the environment. Our environmentally friendly approach is socially focused in that we support local farmers and community organizations.

Our focus is placed upon:

Supporting Small Farmers

We empower small and medium farmers in remote areas, encouraging them to work together while providing them with access to international markets.

Training and Education

We provide training and support for inputs, including seeds and plant protection products, allowed in organic cultivation.

Social Impact

3% of our income is dedicated to supporting local organizations, youth empowerment, and women at risk.

Community Engagement

We engage with local farming communities to educate them about market trends, organic standards, environmental protection, and sustainability.
