Welcome to Bioalba Group

Cultivating Organic Excellence for a Sustainable Future

Bioalba Group is a community of dedicated small and medium sized farmers working relentlessly to produce and cultivate the finest organic herbs, flowers, leaves, roots, and more. With a primary focus on producing quality more than quantity, we are committed to promoting organic excellence while contributing to a sustainable and socially responsible future.

We are an environmentally friendly company exporting predominantly to the European market, and the only company in Albania focused 100% on organic production.

Bioalba is more than just an organic medicinal plants producer and exporter – Our environmentally friendly approach is socially focused in that we support local organizations, local farmers and their families.

We represent a group of organic farmers and pickers who share similar values to ours and similar objectives in securing a wide variety of organic products, which are highly valued by our international partners.

To maximize yield and efficiency, we gather small farmers into groups, allowing them to pool their resources and cultivate larger parcels collectively. This approach helps small farmers increase their income and contribute to the organic market.

With a diverse portofolio of over 100 products coming from cultivation and wild collection practice.

Our focus is on bringing wild collection plants into the cultivation practices in order to ensure quality, quantity and continuity (sustainability).

Our Journey

Established in 2019, BioAlba has been on a remarkable journey of growth and impact. Our dedication to organic cultivation is unwavering, and we proudly stand as the sole company in Albania dedicated entirely to the organic production, providing products for a range of reputable companies across Europe.




The BioAlba Difference

NOT “ Just Organic”


A Community of Organic Farmers
We represent a diverse group of organic farmers who share our values and objectives and are leaded by passion for cultivation. Together, we work towards securing and producing products that are in high demand in today's market.
From Wild Collection to Cultivation
Our produce stems from both careful cultivation practices and the wild collection of precious botanicals. Our organic farmers are also our wild collection pickers.
Remote areas development
Our small farmers and wild collection operators are direct beneficiaries of our production system and trading activities. Purchasing our products means having a direct contribution to our social program linked with families and farms in remote areas. Our work is a financial help for vulnerable families especially women in areas we operate.
A Trustworthy Management Team
With a reputable and experienced management team, we bring trustworthy credentials in the organic sector at both national and international levels.
Traceability and Quality Control
We are meticulous about traceability and act as guardians of our product from the field to the client. Implementation of Internal Control System makes possible the control and traceability of products in every link of the movement from cultivation to export.

Other Services

Besides the production and sale of organic products (cultivation and wild harvesting), Bioalba offers consulting services to guide and assist farmers and clients in implementing organic regulations. Our renowned experts help farmers create and implement management systems that meet the required standards in the market.

Where We Operate
