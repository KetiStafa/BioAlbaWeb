Products

Our products include Folwers, Herbs, Leaves, Roots and Other Diverse and originate from different areas of Albania. Our main storage facilities are based in the municipality of Kamez and in Kerraba, in the outskirts of Albania's capital Tirana.

Quality Control

Rigorous Internal Control System

Quality assurance is at the core of our operations. We've established an internal control system that includes inspections, evaluations, and laboratory testing. This system ensures that our products consistently meet organic requirements and align with client specifications, upholding the highest standards of excellence.

At BioAlba, we are more than just an organic medicinal plants producer; we are committed to making a positive impact on communities and the environment. Our environmentally friendly approach is socially focused in that we support local farmers and community organizations.
