Welcome to BioAlba Group – We are a community of dedicated small and medium sized farmers and pickers, working relentlessly to cultivate and collect the finest organic herbs, flowers, leaves and roots.

With a primary focus on producing quality over quantity, we are committed to promoting organic excellence while contributing to a sustainable and socially responsible future of people’s lives. We are the only organic medicinal & aromatic plants producer based in Albania focused 100% on organic production. We offer a diverse portfolio of over 100 products acquired from cultivation and wild collection practices.
